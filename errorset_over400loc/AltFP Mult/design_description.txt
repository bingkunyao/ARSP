- **Mantissa Multiplication:** The mantissas of the input operands are extended and multiplied together. The result may require normalization (shifting and adjusting the exponent) to fit into the mantissa field of the result format.

- **Exponent Calculation:** The exponents of the inputs are added together, and the bias is subtracted to get the result exponent. The bias is defined by the floating-point format and is necessary to represent both positive and negative exponents.

- **Special Cases Handling:** The module includes logic to detect and correctly handle special cases such as inputs or results being zero, infinity, NaN, underflows, and overflows.

- **Pipelining:** To meet timing requirements and improve throughput, the module implements optional pipelining. The depth of the pipeline is configurable through the `pipeline` parameter.
